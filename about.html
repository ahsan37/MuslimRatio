Hey!
I'm Ahsan.

So, you're interested in me ;)

I'm a student seeking autonomy, discomfort, knowledge, and excitement.

Whether it was solving a Rubix cube, starting a podcast, video editing, or even making this website, Youtube has given me an infinite return on my investment. Like many people my age, I don't know what I want to do with my life, but through online education and compound interest, I'm seeking autonomy.

For the past three years, I've been going through a rebranding phase: seeking an identity and reinventing whenever I got too comfortable. Through my own uneasiness, outdoor adventures, and uncomfortable heights, I am seeking discomfort.

I wanted to be the kid who was exceptionally great at one thing, so I started to read about others who were. Naturally, I fell in love with reading, but the more I learned about these exceptional people and their exceptional ideas, the less I felt I knew. Through people, reading, and experience, I am seeking knowledge.

Wealth, health, and happiness - the beautiful trifecta of life. "Many seek it in that order, but the importance is in reverse" - Naval Ravikant. Through uncertainty, fear, and thrill, I am seeking excitement.

My name's Ahsan Waseem and I'm 20 years old. I was born in Houston, Texas, and have been living here ever since. I have an older brother, and both my parents came to America from Pakistan about 25 years ago. Feel free to follow me on Instagram and Twitter!
